Baruch Hashem, the bochurim of the Beis Medrash have been returned to their proper seats. The first Seder is restored.

But word has reached you of a graver matter. Deep beneath the Beis Medrash lies the Genizat HaChachmah — the legendary Archives of the Sages. Here, the great Rav Yochanan HaSandlar catalogued the works of every Torah author across every era into a meticulous relational database: the Authors table and the Scrolls table, linked by a sacred author_id.

But the two tables have become disconnected! Without JOIN queries to unite them, no one can tell which sage wrote which scroll. The bochurim cannot find the right sefer for their learning. The Shas is unshelved!

Rav Yochanan approaches. "A lamdan must see the connections between things," he says. "Just as every sugya connects to another through a shared svara, every table connects through a shared key. Master the JOIN, the GROUP BY, the HAVING — and you will restore the Archives."
